VU6P Station Equipments

 

Main operational rig will be ICOM IC7100 and additionally, IC-718, Kenwood TS-550S and a SDR based on Peaberry (VUSDR by VU2ZAZ), capable of all band operation (QRP 5 Watts). We may use digital modes on higher bands.
Balun 4:1 (Red toroid) for connecting antenna tuner to 300 Ohm homebrew ladder line (with 320 electrical wire) and TV ribbon cable
Balun 1:1 (Ferrite FT-200-60) for usign at the feed point of W8010 or Fan dipole.
 IC-2200H for VHF - If ducting is good, we could reach lot of VHF operators through VU2TCD repeater having a reach radius of up to 300 miles.
  • T Type Antenna Tuner assembled inside Aluminum Cash Box
  • Air Variable Capacitors - commercial ones 1.5 mm spacing isolated from the box
  • Silver wire used for tuning coil - diameter 3 inches, 14 taps into a heavy duty rotary switch. Wire is 1.5 mm dia.
  • Rig to tuner - RG 213 , connector - SO 239
  • Tuner to Antenna - posts for connecting balanced antennas/tuners and also SO239 for connecting RG 213 with PL 259 (unbalanced). Balun can be connected at the antenna feed point in this case.
  • This tuner is mainly for W8JK we plan to use in the light house operations
  • A Clone of W8010 home made - Has served at home qth for several years.
  • Currently fed by TV Ribbon cable (experimental) with a 4:1 balum at the antenna tuner end.
  • Better configuration would be to just use 50 ohms RG-213 with 1:1 balun at antenna feed point.
  • Rig to tuner - RG 213 , connector - SO 239
  • Maximum performance in 40 meters.
  • Planning to use it as standby or second transmitter
Multiband (40M, 20M and 10M fan dipole with coaxial cable balun was used in last year Lighthouse weekend activities. Had good results with it. This year this antenna will be used as spare.

Hygain tape doublet antenna lying around for several years in my junkbox. Had to open it up and clean up the contacts. It is definitely vintage, came from military junk. I will be trying this antenna in this lighthouse event. Will probably use the 1:1 balun with this.
