There was also another topic in our group regarding evolution – a contention that scientists and those of us who are very educated in the western style would subscribe to too. Evolutionists say that complex organisms evolved from simpler ones. I had told earlier that this is improbable even in the case of a single celled organism. For example, a bacterium to evolve into a bacterium with flagellum (a spiral hair which helps the bacteria move), the probability is incomprehensibly low when calculated mathematically. A flagellum is driven by a cellular motor (you can search it in the web) which spins like a real life motor. Could such an incredible machine be evolving by itself? It makes sense to believe that a more intelligent being creates lesser capable and evolved beings doesn’t it? This exactly is what is taught by our philosophy. It is not hard to imagine that that a spirit soul or Jiva “evolves” over successive births as it passes through more evolved bodies. Once it enters the body of a human, it gets profoundly powerful mind, buddhi and chittha to really analyze, hear and understand the purpose of life, which is to get rid of the birth cycles. A child evolves into a man. A student evolves into a great musician…… Meaning of evolution should be this rather than evolution of “bodily structures”. Pinnacle of evolution is to reach a stage where a body is no longer a requirement as bodies are degradable, destructible and painful – yantra rudani mayaya. Body is just a machine made of “energy” which gets dismantled and dispersed after its time.

So…. Having said that evolution is a process for a jiva soul to arise through a successful platforms of physical bodies to get into a human form, within which they evolve mentally and spiritually to get released from the material bonding, let us see how.

 

Human body is built specifically to receive, assimilate and transmit life energies. Discussion on what these energies are is a separate topic by itself. Human body has a visible physical gross body and subtle spiritual body which becomes visible only when one develop clairvoyant powers. It is in the subtle body that the most remarkable things happen. This becomes accessible to those who practice yogic techniques and by being strict in diet and habits. The energies enter, leave, circulate and transmit through nodal centers in the subtle body. These nodal centers’ development is completely based on a person’s mental evolution. The lowest nodal center is responsible for the basic survival aspect of the human organism – in a child, this is probably the only nodal point which is developed. A child’s only motivation is to eat and survive. Mental faculties are not developed at this stage. If this is the case in an adult human, the person will be severely depressed, suicidal and having no motivation to meet people, even eat and survive. Next higher nodal center is associated with emotions and desires of procreation. Love at this stage is purely lust and animalistic – we haven’t reached the title of this essay yet. Next higher nodal center is associated with power, will to do things – achieve, self-esteem, ego etc…Person with this evolvement level will try to establish a career, income, sustenance, power etc. Even at this stage, the person is like the neighbors in Indian apartments – fight for parking, water, amenities and analysis of whose children is most adorable and so on. Most people end their life at this stage. Next higher nodal point is where we come to our topic – anbe sivam. This point is associated with real love, acceptance. To a person who has crossed over this, a whole new paradigm opens up. This nodal point is perhaps the toughest to cross – the க்ரந்தி (knot) is so tight that it is not easy to open it up here in this nodal point. By being close and in loving nature with himself, other humans and even other life forms, the person feels close to God. This is how அன்பே சிவம் came about. There are further steps to evolution though – the next higher nodal point – communication, creativity, invention, so on and so forth. At this level of evolution, the person will be able to convince, deliver speech, direct other people and create better world and products. Next higher level nodal point is associated with imagination, mental power, clairvoyance, intelligence, intuition, occult and so on. At this level of evolution, the man begins to think all the developments in previous stage are fine but there is something lacking. He starts searching for the ultimate happiness and the source of this universe, “which” is also the source of happiness.  The final Nodal point to open gives the person complete knowledge as the Jiva itself is made of knowledge (ஞானஸ்வரூபீ).  Person can receive cosmic instructions, establish supernatural contacts, transcend consciousness and enter a state a fullness.
