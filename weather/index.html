Very unusual formation for this time of the year. In my 15 years of work in receiving from weather satellites and predicting weather based on both traditional and scientific ways, I have not seen this big a system in Bay of Bengal in this season. With Agni Nakshathra starting May 4, this is a shocker. In the last 60 years, such big cyclone has come only 4 times in this season.
As of now, the system is strenghthening into a cyclonic storm and is poised to move north west towards North Tamil Nadu, Andhra Coast. I will give it a low probability score as of now but if it happens, it is going to be a very destructive one. We would be able to tell more in due course.
